# Contributing to PwnClaw

Thanks for your interest in PwnClaw! Here's how you can help:

## Report Bugs
Open an [issue](https://github.com/ClawdeRaccoon/pwnclaw/issues) with steps to reproduce.

## Suggest Attack Categories
We're always looking to expand our attack library. If you know about new AI agent attack vectors, open an issue tagged `new-attack`.

## Blog Contributions
Have expertise in AI security? We welcome guest posts on [pwnclaw.com/blog](https://pwnclaw.com/blog). Email team@pwnclaw.com with your pitch.

## Security Issues
See [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
